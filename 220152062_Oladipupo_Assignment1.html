About the Conference

Join us for the Black History Conference 2025, an inspiring event dedicated to celebrating the rich history, culture, and contributions of Black communities worldwide. This year's theme, Black Brilliance, highlights the resilience, creativity, and impact of Black leaders, scholars, and change-makers across generations.

Through thought-provoking discussions, engaging workshops, and powerful keynote speeches, we aim to foster education, empowerment, and meaningful conversations about Black history and the future.

Featured Speakers

  • Sandy Hudson - Activism, Black Lives Matter, and Community Organizing
  • Dr. Chika Stacy Oriuwa - Breaking Barriers in Medicine and Mental Health
  • Dwayne Morgan - The Power of Spoken Word and Black Canadian Identity

What to Expect

Dynamic Speakers - Hear from historians, activists, entrepreneurs, and cultural leaders.
